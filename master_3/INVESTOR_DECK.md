## The Technology: "The Kitchen Sink" Architecture
Our proprietary pipeline integrates three distinct layers of intelligence:

### 1. Granular Feature Engineering (The "Micro" Layer)
We do not rely on simple averages. Our engine calculates:
*   **PEAR (Pace-Elasticity & Attrition Response)**: Quantifies a fighter's cardio and durability dynamically.
*   **Chin Health Decay**: Models the cumulative neurological damage and recovery curves for every athlete.
*   **Dynamic Elo**: A custom rating system that rewards finish types and quality of opposition.

### 2. Temporal Sequence Modeling (The "Macro" Layer)
Standard models view a fighter as a static set of stats. FightIQ sees a **trajectory**.
*   **Technique**: We utilize a proprietary **Recurrent Neural Network (RNN)** architecture to encode the "momentum" of a fighter's last 5 performances.
*   **Advantage**: Detects "washed" veterans and "surging" prospects *before* the market odds reflect reality.

### 3. Siamese Neural Networks (The "Matchup" Layer)
We don't just predict "Fighter A vs Fighter B". We model the **interaction**.
*   **Architecture**: Twin neural networks that learn a high-dimensional embedding space where "style makes fights".
*   **Outcome**: The model understands stylistic mismatches (e.g., "Wrestler vs Striker") that traditional XGBoost models miss.
